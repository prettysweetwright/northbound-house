Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Northbound House ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website northboundhouse.com and related product sites. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our sites or use our contact form.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you fill out our contact form, including:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

We also collect standard technical information automatically when you visit our site, such as your browser type, IP address, pages visited, and referring URLs. This data is collected via standard web server logs and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages
  • Communicate with you about our products and ventures
  • Improve our websites and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Email Waitlists

For products listed as "Coming Soon," we offer optional email waitlists. By submitting your email address, you consent to receiving launch announcements and updates about that product. You may unsubscribe at any time via the link included in each email.

Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process contact form submissions. Formspree may store form data on its servers in accordance with its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io.

Cookies

Our sites may use cookies for basic functionality and analytics. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies, though some parts of the site may not function properly as a result.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address below.

Children's Privacy

Our sites are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our contact form.
